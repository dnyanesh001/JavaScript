Experience the beauty of mountain landscapes with our interactive parallax journey. As you scroll, watch how different elements move at varying speeds, creating a sense of depth and immersion.

How It Works

This effect uses multiple layers moving at different speeds as you scroll, creating an illusion of depth. The background moves slower than the foreground, simulating a natural perspective shift.

Smooth Animation

Every element is carefully animated using CSS transforms and transitions, ensuring smooth performance across all devices. The parallax effect responds dynamically to your scroll position.
